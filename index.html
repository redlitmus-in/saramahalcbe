Everything You Need to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the maximum seating capacity for your halls?
Our Grand Celebration Hall can comfortably seat up to 1,200 guests, and the Classic Ceremony Hall accommodates up to 200 guests. Both can be arranged in various styles.
How do I book a date at Sara Mahal?
To book your desired date, we recommend contacting us as early as possible. You can inquire about availability by calling us or submitting an inquiry through our contact page.
Can I bring my own caterer or other vendors?
While we offer excellent in-house catering, we do allow external vendors, provided they are licensed and approved by our management.
Do you facilitate traditional Tamil wedding rituals?
Absolutely! Sara Mahal is renowned for hosting traditional Tamil weddings. We provide spaces suitable for various rituals and can assist with arranging priests.
What are your payment terms?
A deposit is required to confirm a booking, with the balance due before the event. We will provide a clear breakdown of all terms.
