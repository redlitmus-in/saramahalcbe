Frequently Asked Questions

What is the maximum seating capacity for your halls?
Our Grand Celebration Hall can comfortably seat up to 1,200 guests, and the Classic Ceremony Hall accommodates up to 200 guests. Both can be arranged in various styles.
How do I book a date at Sara Mahal?
To book your desired date, we recommend contacting us as early as possible. You can inquire about availability by calling us or submitting an inquiry through our contact page. A confirmed booking requires a deposit and a signed agreement.
Can I bring my own caterer or other vendors?
While we offer excellent in-house catering, we do allow external vendors, provided they are licensed and approved by our management. Please discuss your vendor choices with us.
Do you facilitate traditional Tamil wedding rituals?
Absolutely! Sara Mahal is renowned for hosting traditional Tamil weddings. We provide spaces suitable for various rituals and can assist with arranging priests and customized catering menus.
What are your payment terms?
To confirm a booking, an advance deposit is required. The balance payment is typically settled a certain number of days before the event date. We will provide a clear breakdown of payment schedules upon booking.

At Sara Mahal, we’re dedicated to providing high-quality, personalized event experiences. Our skilled team uses modern amenities to ensure comfortable, efficient, and beautiful celebrations for life's most important moments.
